  • Set Up

    Tiles are laid out in a 1:3:5:7 formation representing a pyramid shape so that there is one in the first row, three in the second row, five in the third row, and seven in the fourth row.


  • How to Play

    - Two players will take turns picking up at least one card.
    - On your turn, choose a single row, and take as many cards from it as you like.
    - Continue picking up cards until all are gone.
    - The player who picks up the last card loses the game.
